I want to help others to make the right decisions, data-driven decisions

As a data analyst and data visualization specialist, I translate data into valuable and comprehensible insights. My goal is to improve results, make the right decisions and save costs.

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed to solve puzzles. So that’s how I look at big data sets: to me it is one big puzzle I want to solve. Finding patterns nobody else sees is the challenge to me.

Before I started as a tutor for data analytics, I had worked for more than three years as a data analyst in the beer & wine industry. This gave me the experience necessary to translate complex questions into understandable insights.

I am experienced in machine learning, supervised and unsupervised algorithms and use data visualization techniques to present the results. I use SQL, MongoDB, Python, JavaScript and Tableau for my data projects.
